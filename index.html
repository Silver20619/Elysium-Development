Development Updates

Early-January : Revamping of original concept.

Previously, the world of Elysium was designed with a closed-concept in mind, but now, we've fully tranferred to an open-expansive world for you and your friends to explore. This open world is planned to include multiple regions on launch as well as secret dungeons for you to explore!

Posted on: January 12, 2025

Mid-January: Building the World.

As of now, multiple functions have been implemented into the world such as an inventory system, loot randomizer, and various UI element scripts. The majority of these scripts can be found in the GitHub repository under the folder scripts.

Posted on: January 15, 2025
